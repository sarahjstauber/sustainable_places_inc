Our Sustainable Future

Since 1985, Sustainable Places® has been dedicated to sustainable, healthy Landscape Architecture, Urban Design, and Architecture. We can help you with expert design, construction, and maintenance of beautiful landscapes, homes, dependable building repair & renovation. We specialize in solving the most challenging stormwater and flooding problems. Our designs provide the best value because we consider all of the "Specialties" that make places work sustainably.

You do have choices. Each building, each landscape is important. By creating places that work with Nature, our designs moderate climate, lower maintenance, alleviate flooding, restore soil fertility, and store carbon. We create value and save you money. From the smallest home and landscape to large-scale planning, Sustainable Places® is proud to help make positive environmental change with beauty and elegance.
